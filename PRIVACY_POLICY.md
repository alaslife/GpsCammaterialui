# Privacy Policy for GPS Camera (MD3 GPS Camera)

**Last updated: July 1, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how GPS Camera ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

GPS Camera is developed with ❤️ in India by **Alas** as a secure, local-first utility.

---

## 1. Information We Access & Process

To provide GPS watermark stamping, the App requests access to sensitive permissions. All processing is done **entirely locally on your device**:

- **Location Data (GPS coordinates)**: The App reads your location coordinates (Latitude, Longitude, Altitude, Bearing, Accuracy) and requests reverse-geocoding to resolve your physical address. This information is written directly onto the pixels of your captured photos and videos.
- **Camera Access**: Used to interact with your device's camera hardware to take photos and record video files.
- **Microphone Access**: Used to record audio during video captures (if enabled in settings).
- **Storage Access**: Used to save your geotagged photos and videos directly to your device's public `DCIM/Pictures` storage and load them in the local Gallery.

---

## 2. No Data Collection or Sharing

- **Local Processing**: We do not host any remote databases. All photo downsampling, video formatting, and watermark compilation are performed locally.
- **Transmission**: The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit your files, location data, or personal details to us or any third parties. 
- **Map Tiles**: The App requests map tiles from public servers (Google Maps and CartoDB) to render standard, satellite, dark, or grey roadmaps on your stamp. These requests only transmit coordinates and map viewport settings; no identifying user information is sent.

---

## 3. Data Security & Control

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on your device settings. You can wipe all local geotagging history at any time by selecting **"Reset Application Data"** inside the App settings.
